Skin type of People

People’s skin can be classified into different types based on oiliness, dryness, and sensitivity. Normal skin is balanced, smooth, and rarely experiences problems, while oily skin produces excess sebum, making it shiny and prone to acne. Dry skin lacks moisture, often feels tight, and may appear flaky or dull. Combination skin has both oily and dry areas, typically oily in the T-zone and dry on the cheeks. Additionally, some people have sensitive skin, which reacts easily to products or environmental changes. Understanding your skin type is important for choosing the right skincare routine and maintaining healthy, glowing skin.
